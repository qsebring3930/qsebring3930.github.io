Plastic Women crowd in front of PepsiCo in protest of plastic usage.

Today, August 31st, 2021, sixty-thousand Plastic Women gathered in front of the PepsiCo headquarters to protest the usage of plastic in their products. Namely, the hundreds of thousands of plastic bottled products that PepsiCo produces on the monthly. In the words of the plastic women, "PepsiCo has no right to use plastic for manufacturing purposes without due recompensation of it's negative effects on the environment to the Plastic Women." The crowd remained peaceful and as of now PepsiCo has not released a response on whether or not they will continue to use plastic in their products.
